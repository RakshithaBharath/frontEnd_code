About Us

  1. Company Mission:
    • Amazon aims to be the Earth's most customer-centric company. Their focus is on providing customers with the best possible experience by offering a wide selection of products, competitive pricing, and fast delivery.
  2. History:
    • Founded in 1994 by Jeff Bezos, Amazon started as an online bookstore before expanding into a wide variety of product categories. Over the years, it has grown to become one of the largest e-commerce platforms in the world.
  3. Core Values:
    • Customer Obsession: The primary focus is on customers and their needs.
    • Innovation: Amazon encourages innovation in order to improve services and products.
    • Operational Excellence: Striving for high standards in all aspects of the business.
    • Long-Term Thinking: Making decisions with long-term growth in mind rather than focusing solely on short-term profits.
